who are we

the story so far

Established in 2022 on Dharug land, Electrique Technique’s mission is to provide a high quality, affordable studio to our beloved local music scene within Sydney. From our humble beginnings as bland office space into the dream recording and rehearseal spot handbuilt for us and our friends. With a passion for vintage electronics and unique sounds, our ever-developing space and growing collection of gear has amassed a total life of its own. Delivering a distinctive character and particular flair to all recordings we make; providing a creative haven where anything goes.

With a large variety of genres and talented personalities coming through our doors, we pride ourselves on creating an environment that’s comfortable for everyone to freely creatively express. Our technicians strive to give the greatest expertise in developing, recording, mixing, mastering and flexibility to fit exactly the vision of our artists. You can decide how hands on or hands off we are with any given element of the process.

Whether you’re looking to rehearse, record, overdub some songs, track an ensemble, mess around on some keyboards, experiment hands on with old school gear or anything in between our studio is your place. With unlimited tea and coffee our studio is primed and ready to be your second creative home.
