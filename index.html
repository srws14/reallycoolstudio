who are we

bit of banter for ya

Opening initially in 2022, Electrique Technique is the continuation of a mission to provide a high quality, affordable studio to our beloved music scene in Sydney. From our humble beginnings as a bare rehearsal room, we’ve slowly hand built our studio with love. Our ever-developing space and ever growing collection of vintage gear has a total life of its own, offering a distinctive character and particular flair to all recordings we make whilst providing a creative haven where anything goes.

We are proud to collaborate with local artists across various genres and remain committed to supporting their visions. We are passionate about capturing performances, staying true to the artist's sound, and knowing when to step back. This approach helps create authentic, unique and fresh recordings.

Whether you’re looking to record your band, overdub some songs, track an ensemble, mess around on some keyboards, experiment hands on with old school gear or anything in between our studio is the place to come. From our very walls hand built or our inclusive tea and coffee, our studio is primed and ready to be your second home.
